Recent Work

Small Data Lab - Cornell University Consulting Software Engineer - Jan '15 to Present
New York, NY Website: smalldata.io

"Small Data" is the myriad of digital data traces we each generate everyday. The Small Data Lab is creating the apps and infrastructure to put your small data to good use for you. They create open-source infrastructure and apps to collect and analyze these traces, in order to convert small data into big insight.

  1. Updated their back-end system to a modular, container based infrastructure and continue to maintain production environments. (Docker, SpringBoot, nginx, Ruby on Rails, and Clojure)
  2. Add new features and perform maintenance on the variety of mobile apps they offer. (Android)
MEI Research, Ltd Project Manager and Software Engineer - July '10 to July '15
Edina, MN Websites: pilrhealth.com and meinergy.com

MEI Research focuses on providing technology consulting and development for health researchers. They have partnered with researchers at many universities and hospitals around the country to develop innovative mHealth solutions, as well as developed apps and a data management/analysis platform internally. While there I:

  1. Was technical lead and developer on all client and internal mHealth mobile app development projects. (Android and Appcelerator)
  2. Wrote multiple successful NIH grant and contract applications.
  3. Lead design and development on a back-end, multi-tenant ‘platform as a service’ system that hosts mHealth research studies. It provides data storage, study management, and data analysis tools for an expanding variety of mHealth apps and devices, all through a single investigator web portal. (Groovy on Grails, Jenkins, Vagrant, AngularJS, OpenCPU, and RabbitMQ)
  4. Helped grow the software team from 1 person (myself) to 8 people, including hiring and implementation of Agile/Scrum processes.
